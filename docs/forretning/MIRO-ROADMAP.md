# Monstr 6-Month Roadmap (April - September 2026)

---

## PHASE 1: LAUNCH & FIRST CUSTOMERS (April 2026)

### April 17 — Presentation for Business Leaders
**30-40 service-based business owners**

Notes:
- Convert at least 2 people from the event
- At least 1 for general automations
- At least 1 for consultations
- At least 1 for speed-to-lead
- Prepare a live demo of the speed-to-lead flow (form → instant callback)
- Have pricing ready and clear — make it easy to say yes on the spot
- Collect contact info from everyone, even those who don't convert immediately
- Follow up within 24 hours with personalized messages

### April (ongoing) — First Paying Customers
Notes:
- Onboard first clients manually — learn what they actually need
- Document every friction point in the onboarding process
- Get testimonials as early as possible (even short quotes)

---

## PHASE 2: REVENUE GROWTH (May - June 2026)

### Build Recurring Revenue Pipeline
Notes:
- Upsell existing clients on additional automations
- Start getting referrals from happy customers
- Consider a second presentation or workshop event
- Build case studies from first clients (with permission)

### Product Refinement
Notes:
- Standardize service packages based on what clients actually buy
- Create clear tiered pricing (speed-to-lead / automations / consulting)
- Automate internal workflows that you're doing manually

---

## MILESTONE: 30,000 NOK in the bank — Register AS Company

Notes:
- Hire a startup lawyer
- Set up all necessary legal documents (shareholder agreement, articles of association)
- Register the company with Brønnøysundregistrene
- Set up a business bank account
- Get proper accounting software (Fiken, Tripletex, or similar)
- Register for MVA (VAT) if applicable
- Set up invoicing and contracts templates
- Get business insurance

---

## PHASE 3: SCALING (July - August 2026)

### Scale Sales & Marketing
Notes:
- Refine outreach based on what's worked (presentations, referrals, online)
- Consider hiring part-time help (VA or freelancer) for admin tasks
- Build out website with case studies and social proof
- Start content marketing (LinkedIn, YouTube — you already have video plans)

### Optimize Delivery
Notes:
- Templatize common automation setups for faster delivery
- Create client self-service dashboards where possible
- Build SOPs for repeatable tasks

---

## MILESTONE: 80,000 NOK MRR — Go Full-Time

Notes:
- Give notice at current jobs
- Ensure at least 3 months of runway beyond the 80K MRR
- Set up proper workspace routine
- Order tickets to Bali
- Research coworking spaces in Bali (Canggu/Ubud have great options)
- Make sure all client work can be delivered remotely
- Set up international payment infrastructure (Wise, Stripe, etc.)
- Consider timezone implications for Norwegian clients (Bali is UTC+8, Norway UTC+1/2)

---

## PHASE 4: LOCATION-INDEPENDENT (September 2026+)

### Bali Setup
Notes:
- Get a KITAS or visitor visa sorted (business visa if needed)
- Join the digital nomad community for networking
- Maintain Norwegian business hours for client calls (mornings in Bali = afternoons in Norway)
- Explore Southeast Asian market opportunities
